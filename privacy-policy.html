Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Clippers Ireland collects, uses and protects your personal information when you visit our website, contact us, or place an order with us.

Who This Policy Applies To

For the purposes of this policy, “we”, “us” and “our” refers to:

Clippers Ireland, The Little Farm,
Knockananig, Fermoy
Co. Cork, P61 EP80

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect certain information automatically. This may include details such as your browser type, IP address, device information, pages viewed, time spent on the site, referring website or search terms, and how you interact with our website.

This type of information helps us understand how visitors use our website and allows us to improve the site, monitor performance and protect against misuse or fraud.

We may collect this information through cookies, log files, analytics tools and similar technologies.

When you place an order, request information, contact us, or create an account, we may collect personal information such as:

  • your name
  • billing address
  • delivery address
  • email address
  • phone number
  • order details
  • payment and transaction information
  • any other details you provide when contacting us

Payment details are processed securely through our payment providers. We do not store full card details on our own systems.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • process and fulfil your order
  • arrange delivery or collection
  • provide invoices, receipts and order updates
  • contact you about your order or enquiry
  • provide customer support, repairs or aftercare
  • manage returns, warranties and service requests
  • help prevent fraud and protect our website
  • improve our website, products and services
  • send marketing emails or product updates where you have agreed to receive them

We will only use your information where we have a lawful reason to do so, such as fulfilling an order, responding to your enquiry, complying with legal obligations, or operating our business responsibly.

Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to help the site work properly, improve your browsing experience, understand website traffic and measure the performance of marketing.

Cookies are small files placed on your device. You can usually control or disable cookies through your browser settings, although some parts of the website may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

We may also use analytics tools, such as Google Analytics or similar services, to understand how customers use our website and to help us improve it.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share your information with trusted third parties where necessary to run our business and provide our services. This may include:

  • payment providers
  • delivery and courier companies
  • website and ecommerce platform providers
  • IT and hosting providers
  • analytics and marketing service providers
  • professional advisers, where required
  • legal or regulatory authorities, where we are required to do so

We only share the information needed for the relevant service.

Marketing

If you have agreed to receive marketing from us, we may send you emails about products, offers, updates or services that may be of interest to you.

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by using the unsubscribe link in the email or by contacting us directly.

Your Rights

Under data protection law, you may have the right to:

  • request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • ask us to delete your personal information, where applicable
  • object to certain types of processing
  • ask us to restrict how your information is used
  • withdraw consent where we rely on consent for processing

If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes it was collected, including fulfilling orders, providing aftercare, meeting legal requirements and keeping business records.

Order information may be retained where required for accounting, tax, warranty, customer service or legal reasons.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information and keep it secure. However, no website or online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so we encourage customers to take care when sharing information online.

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” setting. At present, our website does not change its data collection practices when it receives a Do Not Track signal.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business, website, legal requirements or how we handle personal information.

The latest version will be published on this page.

Warranty Information

At Clippers Ireland, we want you to have confidence in the equipment you buy from us. Our products are carefully selected, tested, and supported by our own workshop and spare parts service.

Most products come with a 1-year warranty from the date of purchase. Clippers Ireland weighing equipment and Sezer milking machines come with a 2-year warranty.

Warranty cover applies to manufacturing faults only. The product must be used and maintained correctly, in line with the manufacturer’s instructions. Damage caused by misuse, incorrect tensioning, poor maintenance, lack of oiling, blocked air vents, wear and tear, or missing internal consumable parts is not covered.

For warranty inspections or repairs, customers are responsible for sending the item to our workshop. If the fault is confirmed as a valid warranty issue, we will complete the repair and return the item to you at no charge.

If your product is outside its warranty period, we can still help. We carry a wide range of spare parts and offer servicing and repairs through our workshop.

Please note that blades, combs, cutters, batteries, filters, drive parts, and other wear parts may be classed as consumable items. These are not normally covered under warranty unless there is a confirmed manufacturing fault. Blade life will vary depending on coat type, use, cleaning, oiling, and correct tensioning.

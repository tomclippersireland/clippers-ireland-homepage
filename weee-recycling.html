WEEE Recycling Policy

At Clippers Ireland, we support the safe and responsible recycling of waste electrical items, batteries and lighting products.

When you buy a new electrical product from us, we will take back your old item on a one-for-one, like-for-like basis, free of charge. This means the old item must be of a similar type to the new product purchased. Retailers in Ireland are required to offer this type of WEEE take-back service.

Waste batteries can also be returned to us free of charge on a one-for-zero basis, meaning no purchase is required, provided they are of a type similar to batteries we sell.

You can also recycle WEEE, waste batteries and lighting products free of charge at many authorised recycling centres and collection points throughout Ireland. To find your nearest recycling point, visit WEEE Ireland's recycling locator or MyWaste.ie. WEEE Ireland states that household electrical, battery and lighting waste can be recycled free of charge at hundreds of drop-off points around the country.

Please do not place waste electrical items, batteries or light bulbs in your household waste bins. These items should always be recycled through the correct WEEE or battery recycling channels.

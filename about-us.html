About Clippers Ireland

Professional clippers and agricultural equipment.

Established in 2012, Clippers Ireland has grown from a specialist clipper supplier into one of Ireland’s recognised suppliers of animal clippers and agricultural equipment.

In 2019, we expanded into manufacturing, adding our own range of cattle weigh bars and weigh platforms, built to provide practical, reliable weighing solutions for Irish farms.

Today, our customers include farmers, equine yards, groomers, agricultural shops and home users. We have also been recognised along the way, including being shortlisted for Agricultural Merchant of the Year at the Agribusiness Awards and the JCI Friendly Business Award.

Our focus remains simple: quality equipment, honest advice, dependable aftercare and competitive prices.

Meet the Team

The people behind Clippers Ireland.

Tom Melby

Owner & Director

After a long IT career ending with Microsoft, Tom founded Clippers Ireland in 2012. Swapping software for clippers, he learned the business from the ground up. Responsible for marketing and product development, he is always looking for new products to add to our ever expanding range.

Con O'Callaghan

Technical Manager

With a degree in Technical Engineering and a farming background, Con brings engineering knowledge and farming experience to the team. Con was integral to the design and development of our livestock weighing system, and remains responsible for their ongoing manufacture.

Mary Fenton

Office Manager

Mary comes from a farming background and has her own smallholding. She eats, sleeps and breathes horses and cattle, and brings that practical knowledge to assist customers every day. Responsible for logistics and ensuring everything keeps moving behind the scenes, Mary is the nerve centre of Clippers Ireland

Gianna Bettiol

Accounts

Gianna looks after accounts and customer support, as well as liaison with our endorsers, sponsored riders and charities. She plays an important role in supporting the people and organisations connected with Clippers Ireland.

Ken Lomasney

Technical Engineer

Ken repairs everything from ErgoPro clippers to other clipper brands, milking machines and brushes. He also rears livestock and is hands-on during calving, so he understands the importance of reliable equipment when it is needed most. He has a proper fix-anything mindset — when he is not playing darts.

Aiko

Self appointed guard dog

Aiko appointed himself guard dog and takes the role very seriously. He likes barking at customers first, making friends later, and supervising the cows and horses from the office door.

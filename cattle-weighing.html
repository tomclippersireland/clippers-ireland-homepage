Choose your weighing system

Do you just need kilos, or do you want usable data?

Choose the setup that matches how you farm: a simple kilo display system or a smart weighing system that saves animal records and helps manage herd data.

Standard weighing system

ErgoPro Standard Weighing System

A simple cattle weighing system for farmers who just want an accurate kilo reading. Manufactured in Cork, it includes our platform, weigh bars and a straightforward digital clock display, with no app or software required.

  • Simple farm weighing
  • Accurate kilo display
  • Irish-made platform and weigh bars
View Standard System

Smart weighing system

WeighRite Smart Weighing System

A smart livestock weighing system for farmers who want more than just a kilo reading. WeighRite is manufactured in Ireland and uses your smartphone as the display, helping save weights, track gain and manage herd data.

  • Saves weights and animal records
  • Tracks weight gain over time
  • Uses your phone as the display
View Smart System

Watch before you choose

Con explains the difference

Con explains the difference between the standard kilo-reading bundle and the WeighRite smart system, so you can choose the setup that suits your farm.

Which setup sounds like you?

Choose Standard if
  • You just want a simple kilo reading
  • You do not need saved herd records
  • You want the simplest, good-value setup
Choose WeighRite if
  • You want saved weights and animal records
  • You want to track weight gain over time
  • You want EID, Herdwatch or ICBF connections

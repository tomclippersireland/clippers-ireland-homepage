Delivery & Returns

Information about delivery within Ireland, store collection, returns, faulty items and how to send products back to Clippers Ireland.

Delivery

Delivery Within Ireland

We send most orders with An Post Express.

Delivery is free on orders over €100. For orders under €100, delivery is €7.

Orders placed before 1pm Monday to Friday are dispatched the same day for next working day delivery. Next day delivery is usually achieved, but it cannot be guaranteed. Once your parcel leaves our warehouse, delivery is handled by An Post and is outside our direct control.

In most cases parcels arrive on time, but during busy periods, bad weather, public holidays or courier delays, delivery may take 2–3 working days.

Please make sure your delivery address, email address and mobile number are correct when placing your order, as these details may be used for delivery updates.

Larger Items

Larger items are sent by pallet delivery. This includes products such as milking machines, livestock weighing systems and motorised brushes.

Pallet delivery is charged at €50.

Collection from Store

You can also collect your order from our warehouse.

Monday to Thursday: 9:00am – 4:30pm
Friday: 9:00am – 3:30pm

Northern Ireland

Please allow 2–3 working days for deliveries to Northern Ireland.

Delivery Outside Ireland

For deliveries outside Ireland, please contact us before placing your order so we can provide a delivery quote and confirm the best shipping option.

Delivery Delays

We always do our best to get orders out promptly, but courier delays can happen from time to time. If your parcel has not arrived within the expected timeframe, please contact us and we will help track it where possible.

Returns Policy

Returning your Product

If you have ordered an item from Clippers Ireland and it is not suitable, or not quite what you expected, please contact us as soon as possible.

Returns must be made within 14 days of purchase. Items must be returned unused, in their original packaging, and in a resaleable condition. Please include a copy of your purchase receipt or order details inside the parcel so we can process your return quickly.

Unless the item is faulty, the customer is responsible for arranging and paying for the return postage. Please make sure the item is packed securely and clearly labelled before sending it back to us.

We strongly recommend using a tracked courier or postal service. Please keep your proof of postage and make sure the parcel is insured for the correct value, as we cannot be responsible for items that are lost or damaged while being returned to us.

Return Address

Please note: for hygiene reasons, clipper blades, combs and similar grooming items cannot be refunded once they have been used.

Faulty or Damaged Goods

If you receive an item that is faulty or damaged, please contact us before returning it. We will advise you on the next steps and, where appropriate, arrange a replacement, repair or refund.

If an item is returned to us and is found not to be faulty, or if the problem has been caused by incorrect use, lack of maintenance, accidental damage or use outside the manufacturer’s guidelines, the customer may be responsible for return postage or repair costs.

If you are unsure whether an item is faulty, please contact us first. We are happy to help and can often advise you before anything needs to be sent back.

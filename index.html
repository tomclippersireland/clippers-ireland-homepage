About Clippers Ireland

Professional clippers and agricultural equipment.

Established in 2012, Clippers Ireland has grown from a specialist clipper supplier into one of Ireland’s recognised suppliers of animal clippers and agricultural equipment.

In 2019, we expanded into manufacturing, adding our own range of cattle weigh bars and weigh platforms, built to provide practical, reliable weighing solutions for Irish farms.

Today, our customers include farmers, equine yards, groomers, agricultural shops and home users. We have also been recognised along the way, including being shortlisted for Agricultural Merchant of the Year at the Agribusiness Awards and the JCI Friendly Business Award.

Our focus remains simple: quality equipment, honest advice, dependable aftercare and competitive prices.

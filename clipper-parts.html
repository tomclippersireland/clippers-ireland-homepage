Workshop Repairs

From simple servicing to fault checks and repairs, our in-house workshop helps keep clippers, trimmers and selected farming equipment working properly.
We can advise before you send a machine in, so you do not waste time or money on the wrong part.

  • Clipper and trimmer servicing
  • Blade, lead, battery and charger checks
  • Fault diagnosis before repair work starts
  • Honest advice if a repair is not worthwhile

Fast, Clear Repair Process

  1. 1. Tell us what’s wrong

    Send the model, issue and usage details. Photos or a short video can help.

  2. 2. We diagnose and quote

    We check the machine and confirm the likely repair cost before work begins.

  3. 3. Repair and return

    Once approved, we repair, test and return the machine as quickly as possible.

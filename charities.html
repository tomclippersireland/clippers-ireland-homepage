Giving back

Charities

At Clippers Ireland, we believe in supporting the people and organisations who care for animals every day.

Over the years, we have donated clippers and equipment to animal charities, rescue centres and welfare organisations. Many of these groups work with limited resources, so practical equipment can make a real difference when caring for horses, dogs, farm animals and other animals in need.

We also try to support local communities where we can, including charity events, fundraisers and local causes.

Below are some of the charities and organisations we have supported.

DSPCA

Animal welfare & community support

If you are involved with an animal charity, welfare organisation, local fundraiser or community event and think we may be able to help, please feel free to contact us.

We are always happy to hear from genuine organisations and local groups. While we cannot support every request, we do our best to help where we can.

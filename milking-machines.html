About Sezer

Sezer is a Turkish manufacturer of milking and livestock equipment with roots in agricultural machinery dating back to the 1950s. The company is known for manufacturing Turkey’s first mobile milking machine and today supplies milking equipment worldwide.

After visiting several factories, we chose to partner with Sezer for their build quality, in-house manufacturing and long-term support. As the sole distributor for Sezer in Ireland and the UK, we supply genuine Sezer portable milkers with full warranty backup, spare parts and our own in-house workshop.
